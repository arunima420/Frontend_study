Will Byers, portrayed by Noah Schnapp, starts as a recurring but pivotal character in the first season of Stranger Things. He is initially more of a 'treasure' that the adventuring party is trying to find, rather than a fully fleshed-out character. However, from the second season onward, Will's character is promoted to the main cast, and his story is explored in more depth. This includes his experiences in the Upside Down and his relationships with his friends and family. His character evolution is marked by his courage to open up about his feelings, especially to those he cares about the most.

Nancy Wheeler is a central, evolving character in Stranger Things, transforming from a typical teen to a tenacious investigator, aspiring journalist, and fearless fighter against the Upside Down, driven by her friend Barb's disappearance, forming key relationships with Steve Harrington and Jonathan Byers, and often challenging expectations with her bravery and intellect. She's known for her strong personality, growth beyond typical high school tropes, and her complex love triangle, but her defining trait is her relentless pursuit of truth, even if it means confronting interdimensional horrors.





Eleven (El) from Stranger Things is a powerful young girl with psychokinetic/telepathic abilities, a traumatic past as a test subject at Hawkins Lab (subject 011), and a journey from scared runaway to hero, finding family with Mike, Hopper, and friends, developing her powers linked to emotions, and becoming Jane Hopper while fighting monsters from the Upside Down. Her defining traits include immense courage, fierce loyalty, struggles with social connection due to isolation, and a connection to Eggo waffles.
